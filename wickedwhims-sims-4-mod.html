    WickedWhims

    TURBODRIVER's WickedWhims[5] is the primary sex mod for Sims 4 so far. Is a mod that offers animated sex, exhibitionism interactions, relationship enhancements and gameplay improvements. It adds custom mechanics, custom skills, custom traits, custom moodlets, custom whims, custom jobs, custom reactions, support for exhibitionism and sex autonomy interactions, custom story progression and modifications to existing gameplay.[6]

    ATF WW Mods enable WickedWhims for children, toddlers and child pets.

    Impressions

    Animations

    Unlike Sims 3, sex animations made for adults (although it works for loli/shota) stretches the bodies showing horribly deformed during the animations. That is because the models adjusts to the bones and no vice versa.Therefore, animations for loli/shota must be designed specifically from zero for children, or also the original animations converted to fit.

    Stuff

    Installation

    Basically, Sims 4 mods do not need installation, these are automatically implemented when you place the files into your Mods folder (Documents/The Sims 4/Mods) and unimplemented by simply removing them.

    WickedWhims

    1. Download and install WickedWhims properly (Ensure the WW version installed matches the one specified in this thread)
    2. For the Children Mod:
      1. Download the Children Mod
      2. Unzip the Children Mod somewhere, inside you should see a folder ATFChildren
      3. Drag the ATFChildren folder straight into your Mods folder (..The Sims 4ModsATFChildren)
        1. It is MANDATORY that it goes straight into the Mods folder, DO NOT PUT IT IN A SUB FOLDER
    3. For the Toddlers Mod:
      1. Download the Toddlers Mod
      2. Unzip the Toddlers Mod somewhere, inside you should see a folder ATFToddlers
      3. Drag the ATFToddlers folder straight into your mods folder (..The Sims 4ModsATFToddlers)
        1. It is MANDATORY that it goes straight into the Mods folder, DO NOT PUT IT IN A SUB FOLDER
    4. For the Child Pets Mod:
      1. Download the Child Pets Mod
      2. Unzip the Child Pet Mod somewhere, inside you should see a folder ATFChildPets
      3. Drag the ATFChildPets folder straight into your mods folder (..The Sims 4ModsATFChildPets)
        1. It is MANDATORY that it goes straight into the Mods folder, DO NOT PUT IT IN A SUB FOLDER

    Note:If you have installed any Dagansta boy/girl bits, ChurchSPH, or X-Loli mod remove those.

    Animations

    Once you have WickedWhims installed, you are able to implement any animation package.

    1. Download the .package files.
    2. Place the .package files into your Mods folder.
